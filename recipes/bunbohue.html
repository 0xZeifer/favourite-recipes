Bun Bo Hue Recipe

Spicy vietnamese beef noodle soup, my absolute favourite dish in the world

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef shank
  • 1 lb oxtail
  • 1 lb pork hocks
  • 0.5 lb Hue style pork sausage

Broth base and Seasoning

  • Water (inside 8 quart pot, add water to cover the meat)
  • 12 oz chicken broth
  • 6 stalks lemon grass, leafy tops removed, roots smashed
  • 1 yellow onions, large cut in half, to be removed from broth after fully cooked
  • 1.5 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablesppon shrimp paste
  • 1.5-2 tablespoon fish sauce

Aromatics & Color

  • 1.5 tablespoon anatto seeds
  • 1.5 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon shallot, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced

Herbs & Veggies

  • Mint
  • Basil
  • Bean sprouts
  • birds eye chile or jalapeno
  • Lime, sliced

Noodles

  • 7 oz package dried rice noodle, medium or large thickness

Sate (Spicy chile condiment

  • 10g dried thai chile, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 40g shallot or white onio, minced
  • 20g garlic, minced
  • 15g lemongrass, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Korean chile powder (gochugaru)
  • 0.5 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 0.5 tablespoon sugar
  • 0.33 tablespoon salt

Instructions

  1. Clean the meat, add all meat to a stock pot and enough water to submerge, bring to boil. Drain and rinse thoroughly with water.
  2. Add the meat, broth, lemongrass and onions to the pot and fill with water almost to brim and bring to boil. Then drop heat to medium-high heat. Add seasoning.
  3. Let simmer, remove meat when they finish cooking pork should be cooked about an hour and beef 2-3 hours
  4. After all the meat has removed, let it cool and slice to pieces
  5. Add aromatics to pot
  6. Boil noodles
  7. Assemble bowl with noodles and add meat and broth
