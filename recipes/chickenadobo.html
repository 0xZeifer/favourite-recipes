Chicken Adobo Recipe

One of the most famous and delicious filipino dishes

Ingredients

  • 1.5lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 3 garlic clovces, minced
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup white vinegar

For cooking

  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon whote black pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken and marinade ingredients into a bowl for at least 20 minutes
  2. Heat 1 tablepoon oil in a pan and add chicken to hot pan and sear both sudes until brown 1 min each side
  3. Remove chicken from pan and set aside
  4. Add garlic and onion to pan and cook for 1 1/2 mins
  5. Add the remaining marinade, water, sugar, whole black pepper into pan and bring to simmer. Then turn heat down to medium high and simmer 5 mins.
  6. Add chicken back to pan and let simmer uncovered for 20-25 mins. Turning chicken at around 15 mins. Cook until the sauce reduces to thick syrup
  7. Serve chicken over steamed white rice and enjoy!
